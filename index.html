About us
Come home to
a tradition of trust.

The illustrious Sandu legacy traces its origins to the 20th century with a foray in the Ayurveda sector.

Our story was inspired by a strong commitment to revive the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda to uplift neglected communities. It has been our constant endeavour to seek new opportunities which can benefit the society at large. In tune with our PHILOSOPHY OF CONTINUOUS EVOLUTION, we expanded our horizons to branch out into the REAL ESTATE sector in the early 21st century. With our strong foundation and revered principles, we at SANDU DEVELOPERS were ready to bring in a breath of fresh air with our beautiful projects and path-breaking innovation.

OUR PROJECTS PORTFOLIO
BUILDING COMMUNITIES

At Sandu Developers we strive to go beyond building beautiful homes. It is our constant endeavour to create strong communities that set an example. In addition to the unwavering commitment towards our consumers, we strongly believe in being empathetic and resourceful to communities that are a part of our surroundings. Success to us goes beyond our projects and extends to our fullfilment of several important economic, environmental and social responsibilities. At Sandu Developers, we are committed to the overall welfare of the communities around us through infrastructure development for the people of our surrounding neighbourhoods.​

A quick glance at our humble initiatives towards fulfilling our philosophy of community development through schools, hospitals, places of worship, environment focussed drives and more.
